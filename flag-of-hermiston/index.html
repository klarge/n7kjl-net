Flag of Hermiston

February 24, 2024 Kristopher Large
design interests

I was born and raised in a small town in north east Oregon called Hermiston, which among few other things is know for it's watermelons. Though I don't actually believe this town needs a flag of it's own I thought it interesting to give it a try:

A tricolour with two stripes shifted under the left third.
The colors represent a watermelon slice and are reminiscent of the Flag of Mexico, a nod to the significant Mexican population in the area. 

3x5 Aspect Ratio
#386640 1/6th, #f2e8cf 1/6th, #bb4748 2/3rd

Simulated flag made with: https://krikienoid.github.io/flagwaver/
