Cleaning Watch Crystal Interior

March 11, 2024 Kristopher Large
interests watches

Out of my various watches, more often than not I end up wearing my Seiko SKX007. However I recently noticed a speck of dust on the inside of the Hardlex crystal, just above the 2 index. I suspect it worked it's way in there the last time fiddled with the 7S26C movement.

I'm an absolute amateur when it comes to working on watches, so none of this should be taken as a tutorial, but it's always fun taking things apart...and try to put them back together again. 

Plan of attack:

  1. Remove bracelet
  2. Remove case back
  3. Remove crown
  4. Remove movement
  5. Dust off the crystal with compressed air
  6. Reassemble
